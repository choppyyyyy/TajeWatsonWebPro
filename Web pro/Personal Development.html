MY STRENGTHS

  • Motivated

    • Throughout the years, I have reviewed my goals and progress on a regular basis. Seeing progress is a great motivator in and of itself, and it boosts my self-esteem. I also continue to set new goals, consider what I want to accomplish in the coming week, month, and year, and tackle one goal at a time to avoid feeling overwhelmed.

  • Focused

    • Over the years I have found that eliminating distractions, reducing multitasking, taking breaks and getting good sleep has helped me to focus better in anything I do.

  • Helpful

    • The most effective way for me to stay helpful is to look around for tasks that need to be completed, whether at home, school, work, or when visiting a friend. Rather than waiting for someone to ask, I'd take the initiative and do it myself!

Don't let others set your goals for you. Pillar 5- We must all endure one of two types of pain: disciplined pain or regretful pain. The difference is that discipline is measured in ounces, whereas regret is measured in tons.
